PB&J

Description:

A Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich is enjoyed by picky eaters world wide!

Ingredients:

  • Two slices of your favorite bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Any Jelly you prefer

How to Make:

  1. Place both pieces of bread on a plate
  2. Slather peanut butter generously and smoothly across one slice
  3. Spread jelly across the other slice
  4. Combine both slices such that the peanut butter and jelly are in contact with one another
  5. Enjoy!
