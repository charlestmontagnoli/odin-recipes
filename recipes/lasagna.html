LASAGNA

Description:

Lasagna is a meal which is beloved by all orange cats around the world. If you want to learn how to make lasagna, follow our recipe!

List of Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 8.5" x 11" Baking Dish

Steps to Make Lasagna:

  1. Preheat Oven to 350°
  2. Line bottom of backing dish with a light coat of pasta sauce
  3. Layer sheets of lasagna sheets with layers of pasta and cheese until dish is filled
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
